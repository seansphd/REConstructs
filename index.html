Results

Each artwork was rebuilt twice on the same model (Claude 3.5 Sonnet) under fixed settings: a Baseline version and a Systems-Situated version. A researcher scored three criteria for each build — algorithmic logic, visual clarity, and user interactivity — on a 1 to 5 scale, giving a total out of 15. The AI then completed the same rubric as a self evaluation. The totals shown on this page are the final-row figures from the Chapter 6 tables, with AI totals for Modules/Structures/Relationships and Designing Letter-like Shapes added from your corrections.
